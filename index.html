About NADDPOD GREATEST STORY OF ALL TIME

Naddpod is a really awesome and cool dungeons and dragons actual play podcast. Essentially a group of people (Emily Axford, Caldwell Tanner and Jake Herwitz) all sit around and record themselves playing DND with their dungeon master (Brian Murphy). And you've probably heard of what dnd is by now. So. Naddpod is split into 3 main campaigns and a few bonus mini-campaigns. The main arcs make up the bulk of the podcast, with mini-campaigns set in-between them, usually acting as filler content for haitus/vacations/breaks The 3 main campaigns are: Bahumia, Eldermourne, and Ba-two-mia. I'll talk about Campaign 1 & 3, which are set in the same world and also the ones I'm crazy crazy bone crazy about

Bahumia is the story set after the campaign. The three legendary heroes who saved the world once before have disappeared, and left the world in total chaos. It's now up to a new band of heroes to defend Bahumia from threats up high and down below the PCS of bahumia are.... MOONSHINE CYBIN - a crick elf who wields both the wrathful power of nature and the fertile power of huge boobs. BEVERLY TOEGOLD THE FIFTH - A city halfling and a proud boyscout of the Green Teens. The only thing that rivals his merit badge collection is his growing collection of dads. Last but not least - HARDWON SUREFOOT bastard of the mountain, the bravest warrior of irondeep. He's a virgin and he isn't over his highschool ex-girlfriend. These 3 heroes will go on to SAVE THE REALM OF BAHUMIA through the power of GOOFS, SCOOPS & ONE BIG BED
